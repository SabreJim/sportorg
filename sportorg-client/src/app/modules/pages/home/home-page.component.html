Sabre Fencing in Fredericton

The Beaches Sabre Club has come to Fredericton and is proud to offer new programs to fencers of all ages and experience. Our head coach Jim Stevens has been teaching fencing for over 20 years and has been building a sabre community in town for the last 10 years.

We have a new facility (or "salle") at our 512 George Street location. It is a little small for now, but we want to grow our space as our club grows. Look for us on top of River Valley Health Solutions which offers great massage therapy and other health services.
